How Profit is Calculated

The profit percentage is calculated using the formula:

Profit Percentage = ((Current Price - Purchase Price) / Purchase Price) × 100

Example:
Current Price = 5000
Purchase Price = 4000
Profit Percentage = ((5000 - 4000) / 4000) × 100 = 25%
