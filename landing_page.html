About Me

My name is Gabriel, a passionate investor in precious metals. For years, I faced the tedious task of manually calculating my investments and their values from different sources. I wanted an easier, faster, and error-free way to track my portfolio—so I built BullionBase. It’s not just a tool; it’s a solution born out of my own experience and desire to make investing accessible for everyone.

Features

Real-Time Data

Fetches up-to-date buy and sell prices for gold and silver directly from VBCE.

Portfolio Management

Add, edit, and track your gold and silver investments with ease.

Wallet Management

Organize your assets with multiple wallets tailored to your needs.

Custom Collections

Add items to specific wallets and view real-time values.

Secure Data

Your data is securely stored using Firebase Realtime Database.

Mobile-Friendly

Fully responsive design for seamless use on any device.

How It Works

BullionBase automates your investment tracking with these simple steps:

Step 1

Add your investments, including quantity, purchase price, and date.

Step 2

BullionBase fetches real-time prices from VBCE automatically.

Step 3

View detailed insights, including current portfolio value and profits.
